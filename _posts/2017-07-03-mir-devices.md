* <b>Project Name:</b>
	* Breathing Games
* <b>Users:</b>
	* Individuals with chronic respiratory diseases
	* Caregivers
	* Passionate professionals
	* Doctors (MD, physicians, specialists)
* <b>The problem:</b>
	* Lack of information
	* Lack of education and healthy habits.
* <b>The outcomes:</b>
